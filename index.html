Our Values and Motto

Vision & Mission

Provide UK-concept international curriculum to even the remotest parts of India at an affordable cost. Align with Sarva Shiksha Abhyan and Beti Bachao Beti Padhao to empower children through education.

Educational Philosophy

Use various teaching methods (oral, written, digital, activity-based) to generate interest in learning!

Curriculum

Adapts British National Curriculum Early Years Foundation Stage to Indian syllabus standards. Emphasizes practical, fun learning with the latest technology.

Facilities

Interactive play area, modern teaching aids, Montessori equipment, and non- toxic play materials.

Parental Involvement

Conducts monthly parentteacher meetings to actively involve parents in their child's early education development.

Safety & Security

Implements biometric attendance, CCTV surveillance, and follows the Ashoka Childcare Policy to ensure non-discrimination

about us

London Kids Preschool aspires to democratize international education, merging UK curriculum with Indian standards. Their vision extends from rural to urban India, aligning with national missions. With 800+ centers and a focus on affordability, they emphasize tech-integrated, practical learning. Expansion plans aim for 2000+ franchise centers nationwide, leading towards global outreach
